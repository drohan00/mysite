Seattle seahawks

Who they are

The Seahawks are a team in the National Football league, they are in the NFC west represent the city of Seattle. Currently coached by Pete carroll.

Achievements

  • The Seahawks are 1x Super Bowl champions (2013)
  • They are the first post-merger expansion football team to appear in consecutive Super Bowls
  • The Seahawks are 3x NFC Champions (2005, 2013, 2014)
  • The Seahawks are 10x winners of their division:
    AFC West: 1988, 1999 NFL West: 2004, 2005, 2006, 2007, 2010, 2013, 2014, 2016
  • Fans of the Seattle Seahawks have twice owned the record for loudest outdoor venue and continue to be recognized by opponents as a factor in football games
  • They are the first team in NFL history to make the playoffs with a losing record (2009)
  • Currently have 4 former players in the hall of fame
    Steve Largent, Cortez Kennedy, Walter Jones, and Kenny Easley

Fun facts

  • Rejected name ideas suggested by locals included “Seattle Running Salmon,” “Seattle Rainbeams,” “Seattle Pachyderms,” and “Washington Georges.
  • Seahawks great Steve Largent officially retired in 1989. Five years later, the wide receiver’s home state of Oklahoma elected him to the U.S. House of Representatives.
  • Sehawks player Shaquem Griffin is the first NFL player to play with only one hand
  • Dan McGwire, the Seahawks’ backup quarterback from 1991 to 1994 was 6’8” tall—the tallest quarterback in NFL history
